How Our Calculator Works

We use scientifically validated formulas to estimate your fitness timeline.

1

Calculate Your TDEE

We use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), then adjust for your activity level to determine Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).

2

Determine Calorie Balance

We compare your current calorie intake to your TDEE to determine your daily surplus or deficit, then factor in your goal pace preference.

3

Calculate Timeline

Based on the calorie gap needed to reach your goal (7700kcal ≈ 1kg), we calculate how long it will take at your current rate.
