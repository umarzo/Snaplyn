Body Hygiene

1. How often do you shower?

2. Do you use soap on all body parts?

3. Do you clean behind your ears, navel, toes, groin, etc.?

4. How often do you change underwear?

5. Do you use deodorant or antiperspirant?

Oral Hygiene

6. How often do you brush your teeth?

7. Do you floss?

8. Do you have bad breath regularly?

9. When was your last dentist visit?

Hair & Nail Hygiene

10. How often do you wash your hair?

11. Do you trim nails regularly?

12. Do you have dandruff or itchy scalp?

Clothing & Environment

13. How often do you wash clothes?

14. Do you wear socks or undergarments more than once without washing?

15. How often do you change bed sheets?

16. Do you keep your room clean and aired out?

17. Do you wash hands before eating?

18. Do you sanitize after touching public surfaces (like bus, cash, gym)?

19. Do you regularly clean your phone and headphones?
